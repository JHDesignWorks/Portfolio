Get to know me!

I pursued graduate studies in Industrial Design at Pratt Institute in New York.
I dive into problem-solving using a bunch of cool tools like Figma, Adobe Suite, Rhino, Keyshot. Not just about pixels, I thrive in teams, rock leadership roles, and bring serious design game to the table. Let's collaborate and create some design magic together!

Outside of design, I'm passionate about reading and meditation. I also enjoy hiking, cycling, and traveling to new places. Recently, I've been captivated by books like '101 Essays That Will Change the Way You Think' and 'My Stroke of Insight'. These readings deepen my curiosity about the intricate connections between our thoughts, emotions, and body. This fascination significantly informs my design approach, inspiring me to create designs that resonate on multiple levels.
