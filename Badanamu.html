Badanamu / 2017

In 2017, I worked as a Social Media Designer at Badanamu, a brand under the UK-based company Calm Island, where I managed social media channels and contributed to the design of the e-commerce website for its early childhood English education VOD service. I used WordPress along with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign to support content planning, creation, and overall brand communication.
